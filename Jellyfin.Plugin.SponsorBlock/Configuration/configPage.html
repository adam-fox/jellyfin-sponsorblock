Paid promotional content
Creator's own products, merch, or services
"Like, subscribe, comment" reminders

Endcards and credits

Tangential content, jokes, or padding

Each user controls skip behavior per-device under Settings → Playback → Media Segments. Jellyfin stores these preferences in the browser's local storage, so they cannot be configured centrally.
